Lancaster

Lancaster, in southeastern Pennsylvania, is known for its Amish community and rich agricultural heritage. The city features attractions like the Central Market, the Fulton Theatre, and Amish farmlands. Lancaster blends historical charm with a vibrant arts scene and picturesque countryside.

Fun facts

  1. Lancaster City, PA, is known for its rich history as the oldest inland city in the United States.
  2. The city's Central Market is the country's oldest continuously operating farmers' market, dating back to the 18th century.
  3. The city's Historic District features landmarks like the Fulton Theatre, one of the oldest continually operating theaters in the United States.

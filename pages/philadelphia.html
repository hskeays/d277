Philadelphia

is Pennsylvania's largest city and is known for its rich history and cultural significance. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art. Philadelphia combines historic charm with a vibrant arts scene and diverse neighborhoods.

Fun facts

  1. Philadelphia, PA, is known as the "City of Brotherly Love" and is Pennsylvania's largest city.
  2. It's home to Independence Hall, where both the Declaration of Independence and the U.S. Constitution were signed.
  3. Philadelphia's Liberty Bell is an iconic symbol of American independence and freedom.

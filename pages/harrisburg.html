Harrisburg

is the capital of Pennsylvania. It is located on the Susquehanna River. Known for its rich history and cultural scene, it features landmarks like the State Capitol and the National Civil War Museum, blending historical significance with modern amenities.

Fun facts

  1. Harrisburg, PA, is the capital of the Keystone State, Pennsylvania.
  2. The city is situated on the banks of the Susquehanna River.
  3. Harrisburg's State Capitol building features a striking dome inspired by St. Peter's Basilica in Rome.

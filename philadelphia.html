Philadelphia

Philadelphia, Pennsylvania's largest city, is known for its rich history and cultural significance. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art. Philadelphia combines historic charm with a vibrant arts scene and diverse neighborhoods.

Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania, known as the Keystone State, is a historically rich state in the Northeastern U.S. It was one of the original 13 colonies and hosted the signing of the Declaration of Independence in Philadelphia. With vibrant cities like Philadelphia and Pittsburgh, scenic Appalachian Mountains, and Amish farmlands, Pennsylvania offers a diverse and unique experience.
